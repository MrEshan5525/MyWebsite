Who is Kalki?

Kalki is the tenth and final avatar of Lord Vishnu, according to Hindu scriptures. He is predicted to appear at the end of Kali Yuga which is mentioned 400K years after, the current age of darkness and corruption.He is also predicted to appear in these centuries, Riding a white horse and wielding a blazing sword, Kalki will annihilate the forces of evil and establish a new era of truth and righteousness, known as Satya Yuga.

Kalki's arrival is mentioned in the Puranas, including the Vishnu Purana and the Bhagavata Purana. He symbolizes the ultimate restoration of dharma (cosmic order) and the triumph of good over evil.
